OUR STORY

MSR is owned and operated by SpaceY, an Australian aerospace manufacturer and space transporation services company based in Brisbane Queensland

The current head of the MSR as well as SpaceY Melon Husk is extremely passionate about MSR, as it has always been his dream to visit and colonise other planets.

Melon Husk then set out to terraform Mars the closest viabily habitable celesetial body in 2055, finalizing the construction and logistics of MSR in 2074.

Thus, from these efforts, he and his team had finally realised a place to where everyone could truly experience the interstellar adventures found only in dreams.
