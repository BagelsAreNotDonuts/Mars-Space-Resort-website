ENJOY YOURSELF AT MSR

Mars Space Resort offers a variety of activities which allow for you to attain the out of this world experience you've only seen in movies!

ZERO-G LASER TAG

Experience the thrill of laser tag without anything weighing you down!

With our high-tech gravity negating technology and next-gen laser tag equipment and facilities you can experience laser tag the way it ought to be!

WALKING TRACKS

"One small step for man, one giant leap for mankind"

Explore the newly terraformed Mars for yourself in all its' glory! And experience in person, the ecosystems and seceneries which will eventually constitute mankind's future!

ZERO-G SWIMMING POOLS

If you feel weightless in water, wait until you feel weightless water!

It's not just Laser Tag we've made gravity free, we've done it to the water too! Experience a whole new way of "swimming" only at MSR!

RACING CIRCUITS

No-G? No-problem!

We've magnetised our tracks so you can experience safely racing carts on Mars just as you can on Earth, but now with the added the verticallity of magnetized loops and more!

LOW-G GOLF

If you thought normal golf was boring, MSR's low-g golf is sure to lift you off your feet!

Beginner or expert, it doesn't matter! Play golf in our special low-gravity, themed courses! Play golf the way it was meant to be played!
